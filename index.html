About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Statistics and Data Science, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), supervised by Prof. Guanhua Chen.

My research interests lie in the intersection of Large Language Models (LLMs) and reasoning. Specifically, I am focusing on improving the reasoning capabilities of LLMs and exploring Multimodal LLMs.
